About Me

I am a PhD Researcher of the University of Edinburgh and University Suor Orsola Benincasa of Naples. My name is Panagiotis Kourtesis. I am PhD candidate (2nd year) in Experimental Psychology and Cognitive Neuroscience of a joint program between University of Edinburgh and University Suor Orsola Benincasa of Naples. My professional and academic experiences in cognitive and clinical neuroscience comprehend telemedicine, cyber-psychology, diagnostic and evaluation neuropsychological assessment, which enables the neuropsychologists to provide a person-centred compensatory rehabilitation. My current endeavour aims to develop the first apps library of Virtual Reality assessment tools.
